Haunt

A ghost game for Linux, Mac and Windows

Source Trello

What is it?

In Haunt, you play as a ghost, trying to destroy the world. You start off without many powers, but slowly build them up.

It is made in Unity, and is supposed to be built for Linux, Mac, and Windows. It might work on other platforms, although there are no guarantees.

What can I do?

As the ghost, you can:

  • Pick things up and move them.
  • Go through certain objects.
  • Possess people.
  • Blast pure energy in all directions at once.
  • Fire pure energy at something.
  • Go invisible.

Haunt is a FOSS (MIT License) game - you can fork and contribute to it here.

In its development, I have used Unity 2017.1.0f3 as the game engine, Blender for 3D models, and the GIMP for textures (or at least what little textures there are!). I use Atom with the omnisharp plugin for writing code.
